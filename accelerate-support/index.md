
# Accelerate

Accelerate is a virtual reality training program for GMP operators, designed around Cell & Gene Therapy Catapult’s manufacturing centre in Stevenage, UK. Accelerate navigates trainees through an immersive GMP compliant manufacturing facility, with standardised, modular content spanning:
- Cleaning
- Gowning
- Compliance
- Health & Safety
- Facility Orientation
The interactive laboratory walkthroughs and simulations provide exposure to a real-life facility, without the risks associated with in-person training.
